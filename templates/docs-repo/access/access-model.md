# Access Model

## Purpose

Document who can access each repository, runtime environment, and operational system.

## Review Cadence

Review access at least quarterly and whenever a team member changes role or leaves the project.

## Minimum Fields

| System | Access Group | Role | Owner | Review Cadence |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| `<repo-name>` | `<group-name>` | read/write/admin | `<owner>` | quarterly |

Do not store private credentials or personal contact details in public documentation.
